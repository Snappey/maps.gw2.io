A fast, friendly map for Guild Wars 2 — built because I love scrolling around maps while wasting time, and none of the others felt nice to use. Smooth panning, every marker you need, and live event schedules for each expansion.

Basics Markers Layers WvW Live Map
  • Move around — drag to pan, scroll to zoom.
  • Draw — hold right-click to sketch, just like in-game. Your line fades after you let go.
  • Share a view — the link follows where you're looking, so just copy the URL. Here's the Isle of Istan.
  • Jump to a chat link — paste one on the end, e.g. https://maps.gw2.io/tyria/[&BO8AAAA=], and a green circle marks the spot.

Shortcuts

  • 1 Layers
  • 2 Live Markers / Matches
  • 3 Events / Match Stats
  • 4 Wizards Vault
  • Esc Close panels
  • Click a marker — copies its chat link to your clipboard.
  • Double-click a marker — opens its wiki page.
  • Big cities like Lion's Arch and Arborstone come with extra detail.
  • World bosses pop onto the map as they're about to start — click one to fly there and grab the nearest waypoint.
  • Events — press 3 for full schedules, grouped by expansion.
  • Open it — from the toolbar, or press 1.
  • Toggle anything on or off with the eye.
  • Search to filter the list fast.
  • Pin a layer to keep it visible at every zoom level.
  • Show all / hide all in one click.

Marker packs

Optional layers from the community. Lady Elyssa's Markers (Bounty, Ranger Pets, Fishing) are courtesy of Lady Elyssa's Markers — flip them on from the Layers panel.
  • Switch to the Mists from the toolbar.
  • Pick a match to load it onto the map.
  • Score bar up top tracks points and per-tick for all three teams.
  • Click an objective — see who owns it, the claiming guild, upgrades, time held and the RI timer.
  • Sector colours show who controls what at a glance.
  • Match stats — skirmish details, full history and a score graph.
